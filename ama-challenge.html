Ama’s Design Challenge

Ama is a Senior High School Visual Arts student who has been given a real project to design the packaging for a new biscuit company. She worked very hard and submitted three packaging samples to the company. Unfortunately, all three designs were rejected! The company said her designs did not reflect the brand identity and would not attract buyers. Now Ama needs your help to find out what went wrong. As an aspiring designer, your job is to analyze Ama’s three packaging designs, identify the problems, and help her select the best colour scheme and design approach for the biscuit packaging?

Design Briefs

Fun-Design Smaple 1
Fun and playful biscuit packaging for kids – choose a color palette that feels lively and energetic.
Trust-Design Smaple 2
Trustworthy and natural biscuit packaging for families – select a palette that communicates freshness and reliability.
Premium- Design Smaple 3
Premium and elegant biscuit packaging for adults – pick a color palette that looks sophisticated and stands out.

Review Ama’s Designs

Select the design you think needs improvement and tell us what you think is wrong with it.

Offline Assignment: Redesign Ama’s Samples

Create three new versions of Ama’s packaging samples (one for each brief). Use paper, markers, or a digital tool. Your teacher will assess these offline.

  • Produce three distinct redesigns addressing the brief for Fun, Trust, and Premium.
  • Annotate each redesign with the changes you made and why.
  • Bring your physical or digital files to class for teacher assessment.
