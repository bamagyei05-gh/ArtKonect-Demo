Balance

What is Balance?

Balance in design refers to the distribution of visual weight within a composition. It helps create stability and structure in artwork or design layouts. There are two main types of balance:

  • Symmetrical Balance (Formal): Elements are arranged equally on both sides of a central axis, creating a mirror image.
  • Asymmetrical Balance (Informal): Elements are balanced by visual weight, not by being identical. Different shapes, colors, or sizes can balance each other.
Example: A symmetrical leaf vs. an asymmetrical bird

Why is Balance Important?

Balance makes designs feel stable, harmonious, and pleasing to the eye. Without balance, a design can feel awkward or uncomfortable.
